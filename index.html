Cognify

AI-Powered Cognitive Impairment Diagnosis

NBER Conference 2026
About

Cognify is a clinical decision-support tool designed for the Medicare Annual Wellness Visit. It uses a hierarchical transformer model trained on 57,000+ patient visits and optionally MRI scans to classify cognitive impairment—from normal cognition through MCI to dementia—and perform dementia subtyping across Alzheimer's, Lewy Body, Frontotemporal, and Vascular dementia, achieving 0.958 AUROC.

Currently undergoing clinical validation at Carle Health (US) and Nanavati Max Hospital (India). Initial results from an 80-patient dementia cohort at Carle show 78/80 correct classifications.
